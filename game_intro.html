Welcome to the Dino Dash Game! 🌋


Embark on a thrilling adventure in the prehistoric world where you take on the role of a daring dinosaur dodging obstacles and leaping over towering stones. But here's the catch – it's not just about survival; it's about scoring big to unlock exclusive discounts!

How to Play:🦕

Press any key to make your dinosaur jump and avoid the oncoming stones. Each successful dodge earns you a point. The more stones you conquer, the higher your score.

Discount Milestones:🦕

🌟 20 Marks: 10% discount !
🚀 50 Marks: 20% discount !
🏆 100 Marks: 30% discount !

Are you ready to dash through the Jurassic landscape, earn marks, and unlock amazing discounts? Let the Dino Dash begin! 🎮🦕
